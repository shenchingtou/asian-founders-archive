    What is an Impact Maker?
    What is an Impact Maker? Archive: March 26, 2018 Questioning + Action = Impact Image courtesy of Impact HUB Tokyo In February 2013, Impact HUB Tokyo had begun renovations a former print factory’s ground floor. After surviving for two years, co-founders Shino and Shingo, painted the words “Questioning + Action = Impact” on the building’s white wall.
